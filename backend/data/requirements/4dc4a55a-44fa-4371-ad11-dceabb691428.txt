Business Requirements Document (BRD)
Project Title: Employee Leave Management System
Purpose: Develop a web-based system to streamline leave requests, approvals, and tracking while
reducing manual work.
Business Objectives:
- Automate leave requests and approvals.
- Reduce administrative effort by 50%.
- Improve accuracy of leave records.
- Centralize leave management.
Scope:
In Scope: Login, leave requests, approvals, balance tracking, notifications, reports.
Out of Scope: Payroll, recruitment, performance management.
Stakeholders: Business Owner, HR Manager, Project Manager, Development Team, Employees.
Functional Requirements:
- Secure login.
- Submit leave requests.
- Approve/reject requests.
- Automatic leave balance updates.
- Email notifications.
Success Criteria:
95% availability, approval time under 24 hours, user satisfaction above 4.5/5, 50% reduction in
manual processing.
